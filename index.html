Learn The Chords

Learn the beginner chords and why you should master them.

Learn The Core Chord Progressions

Learn the chord progressions that are beautiful to the ear. don't waste your time trying to figure out what comes next actualy know it.

Know The Notes On The Kneck

Get to grips with the fretboard and discover where the notes are and how they repeat. Don't think that you don't need to learn this, as soon as you do you will be playing chords all over the neck.

FingerPicking Patterns

Let us show you how fingerpicking can be made easy. Discover methods that are simple to play but sound incredible. Once you have that then progress to more complicated picking and soon you will be wondering what all the fuss was about.

Learn How To Practice

Stop wasting hours and discover how to cram more in each of your practice sessions. Stop repeating the same old things and create some different shapes with your hands and fingers.

Learn Songs All The Way Through

Let us take you and teach you guitar songs all the way through. Yes the whole song, there is nothing worse than someone asking you to play a song and you only know a little bit of it. Actually impress people and let them sing along.

Discover a Chord System

Discover a chord system that allows you to pick a chord and then play it anywhere over the neck. Learn to find every root note so that you can play whatever chord you want off of it.

How to Build a Chord

Learn the theory behind building chords. Discover the fundamental building blocks of our western music system and see what makes a major chord major and how it becomes a minor chord. Build chords by adding extra notes or create suspended chords.
